repair don't replace · rethink everything
the story

in today's world, single-use plastics reign, causing devastating damage to the environment. dagad emerges as your reliable everyday weapon in the battle against plastic pollution — taking on a mission as bold as it is practical: to turn the tide on plastic waste and give new life to something equally responsible for environmental harm — discarded denim jeans.

dagad isn't just a bag; it's a story. crafted from discarded denims, each dagad is engineered to last. rugged looks, ergonomic design, convenient features — the ideal ally for your everyday adventures.

every stitch, every mark, and every variation carries the legacy of its past life, celebrating wabi-sabi — the beauty in imperfection. minor discolorations, scratches, or signs of wear are not flaws; they are the features. they remind us to repair, not discard.

it's not just a bag; it's a conscious choice — a weapon against plastic pollution. carry dagad proudly. with each step, you're making a difference.

take the first step. carry dagad. and together, let's make sure the only thing we're throwing away is the old ways of thinking.
